About Us

Julian Cho Technical High School is a technical high school located 14.5 miles on the San Antonio Road in the Toledo District. We have an enrollment of 975 students. One of our academic pillars is agriculture. We pride ourselves in teaching our students the skills to grow and produce their own food. Therefore, we produce fresh vegetables and meats to supply to our feeding program. With the skills acquired when students leave JC, they can continue to produce their own food in their communities and be self sustainable.
